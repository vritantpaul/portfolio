Welcome!

Hi! I'm Vritant, a self taught data analyst. I have a degree in business administration and I'm a MBA dropout. Why the drop, you ask? Well, short answer: to find my Ikigai. Long Answer: A lack of intrinsic connection with my discipline forced me to look beyond the horizon for other prospects. That's when I stumbled upon the data science job family.

Being a bibliophile, my reading of classics like 'Factfulness', 'Enlightenment Now' and alike opened my eyes towards the vast potential of the data analytics field. As the saying goes, "Data is the new oil", and being a proponent of rationality and factfulness, I believe in this day and age, to be pragmatic is to be data-driven and whether its our lifestyle (the tiny chunks of habits which constitutes our day) or a system as complex as a nation, to monitor progress is to have an eye out for the data that's been constantly generated. Realizations such as this motivated my career transition.

As someone who enthusiastically look forward to the future, for me the ability to analyze data and to extract meaningful insights is an indispensable skill to have. If we are a match, look forward to working with you and the conversations we'll be having.

Projects

Cyclistic Bikeshare Case Study

Cyclistic is a company which offers rental bike services in Chicago. Here I did a descriptive customer analytics to boost cyclistics' membership subscription by converting casual riders to members and came up with insights and recommendations necessary to make that convert possible. The whole analysis is done using Python.

Youtube to Notion Notes

This is a python program which will take any youtube video with subtitles and write its summary in Notion. It starts off with retrieving the transcript of the video using Youtube Transcript API. Split the transcript into several paragraphs and summarize those paragraphs using ChatGPT. And finally write those paragraphs as bullet points in Notion via Notion API.

Bellabeat Fitness Tracker Case Study

Bellabeat is a US based firm of wearable computers for women. Here I did an exploratory analysis on a dataset containing data from 30 participants, having recorded data of their heart rate, steps taken per day, BMI, sleep duration etc. for a span of 1 month. The whole analysis is performed using R programming language.

Ed-Tech Customer Analytics

Here I did a Customer Analytics for an ed-tech firm by zeroing in on their major customer touch points like SEO, social media engagement, email marketing etc. And did a thorough evaluation of KPIs to drive customer engagement. The whole analysis is visualized through Tableau.

Amazon Product Price Drop Notifier

Scarpes given Amazon product pages on a periodic basis. Compares actual price to desired price, and if actual price is less than or equal to desired price shows a windows notification. This program uses Windows built-in task scheduler.

Netflix Titles Analysis

Netflix tops as the number one streaming service provider in today's world. I did an investigative analysis on 8K+ Netflix Titles both movies and TV shows. The analysis is summarized as a Tableau dashboard. Spoiler alert: TV shows are the new thing outperforming movies, significant uprising ever since the inception of COVID and lockdown.

Covid-19: Data Exploration

For this project I did a data exploration on COVID 19 data particularly death toll and vaccination rate across countries. The dataset is provided by Our World in Data. I used SQL queries to answer some key questions and visualized the major findings using Tableau.
